Change of Soul

Nothing seems to make me happy,

Everything nowadays is too sappy,

While I think of my family today,

Even that seems to have become gray;


Jumping up with excitement is gone,

The hearts now seems to be pricked by thorns,

The rush I felt to watch on television before,

It seems to just make my eyes sore;


Now I find no bitterness in their speech,

It seems as if happiness is far from my reach,

Every emotion seems too silent to speak,

The gray world of mine make me feel weak;


All too grey for me to feel,

It seems to me that I will never heal;

~@Vaishnavi Gupta
